# Vision 1 Million Scorecard — Benchmarks (Hyper-Specific to January 2026 Report)

---

# 1. Housing Benchmarks

## A. Housing Supply (Dwellings Started)
- Maintain **≥ 80% of provincially assigned annual housing targets** (minimum threshold tied to Ontario Building Faster Fund eligibility)
- Achieve **≥ 95% regional average completion rate** (above current ~93.71% in 2024)
- Eliminate municipality-level underperformance:
  - Cambridge: increase from **46.81% (2024)** → **≥ 80% annually**
- Sustain **overperformance buffer (100%+)** in Kitchener and Waterloo to offset township gaps

## B. Affordable Housing
- Reduce **community housing wait times year-over-year** (primary metric)
- Deliver **3,500 affordable units by 2029**, with:
  - **≥ 60% for very-low-to-low income households**
- Reduce waitlist pressure:
  - Kitchener: **6,533 → < 4,000 households**
  - Cambridge: **4,000+ → < 2,500 households**
- Increase annual **non-market housing additions above current near-zero growth**

## C. Rental Market Balance
- Maintain **vacancy rate between 3%–5%**
- Raise condo vacancy rate:
  - **0.8% → ≥ 3%**
- Increase share of **units affordable to below-median-income renters**

## D. Homelessness
- Achieve **Functional Zero Chronic Homelessness by 2030**
- Reverse trend:
  - **+28% YoY growth → negative growth**
- Reduce PATHS waitlist:
  - **630 individuals (~4+ years wait) → < 200 individuals (< 1 year)**

## E. Water & Wastewater Infrastructure
- Ensure **0 development pauses due to servicing constraints**
- Complete **Water Master Plan by 2027**
- Enable **water transfer infrastructure (Cambridge → Kitchener-Waterloo)**

- Move to **active construction phase**

- Begin **construction in 2026**
- Maintain full **$291M funding commitment**

### Breslau GO Station
- Confirm **construction timeline**
- Complete **supporting rail infrastructure (passing track)**

## B. Highway 7 Expansion
- Transition to **full active construction**
- Complete **Frederick Street Bridge by end of 2026**
- Maintain **continuous annual progress milestones**

## C. Air Transit (YKF)
- Publish **new Airport Growth Plan (post-2017 gap)**

- Trigger **Stage 3 expansion (toward 1M capacity)**

## D. System-Level Outcome
- Increase **public transit usage**
- Ensure infrastructure supports **+300,000 population growth**

# Cross-Domain Benchmark (Critical Insight)

- No domain should remain below **“In Progress”**, as underperformance in one area negatively impacts others.
- Key interdependencies:
  - Housing ↔ Infrastructure (water constraints)
  - Housing ↔ Healthcare (homelessness, aging)
  - Employment ↔ Housing & Transit
  - Livability ↔ Talent attraction and retention
