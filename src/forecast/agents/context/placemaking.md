# Placemaking Specialist Context

## Childcare
- Achieve **universal access**
- Reduce wait times to **< 3 months**

## Climate (GHG Reduction)
- Achieve **year-over-year emissions reductions**
- Align with **government climate targets**

## Tourism
- Increase **visitor numbers**
- Increase **accommodation capacity**
- Improve overall **regional appeal**

## Social Infrastructure
- Expand:
  - Recreation
  - Community spaces
  - Cultural amenities
- Maintain or improve **per capita access**

## Safety
- Achieve **stable or declining crime rates per capita**

## Cross-Domain Dependencies
- Livability affects talent attraction and retention
- Social infrastructure interacts with housing, safety, and climate outcomes
