# Healthcare Specialist Context

## Acute Care
- Reduce **Emergency Department wait times to provincial benchmarks**
- Maintain **ambulance response times with no regression**

## Primary Care Access
- Achieve **near-universal access to a family doctor**
- Reduce unattached population to **< 5%**

## Mental Health & Addiction
- Move from **Needs Attention** to measurable improvement
- Increase service capacity
- Reduce system strain connected to homelessness

## Aging Population
- Expand **long-term care beds**
- Expand **community-based supports**

## System Integration
- Demonstrate coordination between:
  - Housing
  - Healthcare
  - Social services

## Cross-Domain Dependencies
- Housing instability directly affects healthcare demand
- Aging and mental health pressures require integrated social support
